Background

Select a background with the dropdown below. You will see a free skill level that you will receive upon selecting that background. After that, you will have the choice of two options from the learning table. If you choose 'Any Combat' or 'Any Skill' you will then have the choice of a selection of any of the relevant skills. Edges

Every PC picks two Edges at character creation. Eventually at 5th level a PC will get access to another. They provide special features, extra skill levels, or unique mechanics for the Edge and maybe a Focus. You will select two choices from the dropdowns below, and the relevant details of the selected Edge will be displayed for you.
Note:Unless otherwise mentioned by the Edge, you can take each edge only once.








Focus

At character creation you can pick one Focus normally, however if you took the Focused edge, you will have the choice of two. It could be two different Foci, or two of the same to get the benefits of both levels Some offer additional skill levels, however they cannot raise you above the 2 level max from any other choices or sources of skills. Foci give your character a special ability, or better aptitude. Some improve existing traits, some are completely unique. Most Foci come in two levels, with the second enhancing the effects of the first and offering new skill choices. Budget: At first level the budget from Foci is $5,000 for vehicles, and $1,000 for drones. They grow quickly beyond first level, opening up the choices significantly.
Note:If you plan on taking two levels in the same focus, you MUST select them in the proper order, otherwise it will display incorrectly.

Finishing Touches

Here you will choose a bonus skill, roll your for your hitpoints, and review your saving throws and base attack bonus.
Hitpoints are 1d6 or 1d6 +2 if you have the Hard to Kill edge. Base attack bonus is +0, or +1 if you have the On Target edge. Physical saves are 15 minus the better of your Strength or Constitution modifiers. Evasion saves are 15 minus the better of your Intelligence or Dexterity modifiers. Mental saves are 15 minus the better of your Wisdom or Charisma modifiers.
Note: Only complete this section after your edges and ability scores are finalized.
